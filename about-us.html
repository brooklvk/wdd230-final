About Us

We sell produce directly from farmers in our California area, and we also use national and international (organic!) suppliers to provide exotic fruits.

We welcome our loyal local customers as well as tourists visiting from afar. We believe in healthy eating, organic bounty, and convenience for our customers.

When you make an order, it is delivered via food truck to one of our many pick-up locations between Los Angeles and San Diego.

Our Story

We started out with a two-acre lot planted with strawberries, selling via a fruit stand.
We started as a family business and have grown to a family-owned business with over 50 employees.

Bountiful Foods operates organically, selling fruit from many local farmers as well as our own small plot.
We also provide fruit from ethically sourced international suppliers.
